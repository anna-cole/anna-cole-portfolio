More About Me

After graduating with a bachelor's degree in Law, in Rio de Janeiro - Brazil, I spent some years working in the legal field. Despite having a successful legal career, I wanted more. I wanted to see the world and experience a different culture firsthand, so I moved to the UK.

While enjoying the British culture and improving my English, I started working in logistics. But soon I realized that something was still missing: a creative side of me was not being fulfilled. I needed to bring ideas to life, output elements on a screen, create something out of nothing.

It was when I decided to move to the US and to try something new, so I enrolled in a Software Engineering bootcamp, and a passion for coding was born: nowadays, I don't even imagine myself doing anything else!

While my path to becoming a Software Engineer was non-traditional, my love of programming and technology has motivated me to continually learn and grow in my career. Let's connect and discuss how my skills can contribute to your next endeavor!
